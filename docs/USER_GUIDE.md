# TOP Portal (CDC Türkiye) — User Guide

*Version 2.0 · July 2026 · A formatted Word/PDF version of this guide is in this folder.*

## 1. Introduction

Training is organised as **events (programmes)**: each event contains one or more **sessions**, and every session is its own meeting with its own date, time, platform, meeting link, trainer and category. Trainees are organised in **batches**, each batch is led by a **team leader**, and events can be targeted at specific batches.

| Role | Who it is for | What they can do |
|---|---|---|
| Trainee | New hires taking training | See the events for their batch, register with Notify Me, join sessions, download per-session Outlook invitations, rate trainings |
| Trainer | Employees who deliver training | Create events and sessions (when enabled), add sessions to assigned events, mark attendance, view reports |
| Team Leader | Leads one or more trainee batches | Dashboard with their trainees' progress — registrations, attendance, upcoming sessions |
| Administrator | The L&D team | Everything: events, sessions, trainers, team leaders, batches, trainees, announcements, reports, settings |

## 3. How events and sessions work

- Every session has its own **name**, **trainer**, **category**, **date/time**, and **platform + meeting link** (generated automatically at publish for online platforms).
- **One registration covers the whole programme** — Notify Me registers the trainee for every session.
- **Outlook per session:** each session row has an Outlook button that downloads that session's invitation (link, agenda, trainer, reminder) and turns into a green **Downloaded** tick.
- **Visibility by batch:** events target Everyone or one or more batches; trainees outside those batches can't see or register.
- **Registration window:** optional valid from/until dates; outside the window registration is closed.

## 4. For Trainees

Find trainings on the Trainings page or Calendar (sessions appear as "Programme (S2/3)") → open an event → **Notify Me** → add each session to Outlook with its button → Join from the session row on the day → download materials → rate the training after completion → Cancel registration if plans change.

## 5. For Trainers

- **Create event** → details → add sessions ("Add session", each with its own name/date/times/platform) → Publish or Save as draft.
- **Assigned events:** when an admin assigns you to a collaborative event, it appears in My Events with an "Add session" button; you see all sessions of events you're assigned to.
- Manage with Edit / Duplicate / Cancel / Mark completed; tick attendance in the Participants panel; use filterable, exportable Reports.
- The administrator can switch off trainer event creation in Settings — sessions then appear on your dashboard automatically.

## 6. For Team Leaders

Your dashboard shows your batches' datapoints: trainee count, batches led, registrations, team attendance %, upcoming/completed sessions, a per-trainee table and an attendance-by-batch chart. **My Trainees** lists everyone in your batches with full training history; the Calendar shows sessions your trainees are registered for.

## 7. For Administrators

- **Batches (master):** create batches, assign a team leader per batch, add trainees in bulk (checkbox multi-select with Select all). Deleting a batch clears its leader and members.
- **People:** create/edit/disable/delete Trainers (Trainer@123), Team Leaders (Leader@123, with batch assignment) and Trainees (Trainee@123, with batch).
- **Events:** any trainer per session; "Visible to" batch chips; optional registration window; **collaborative events** ("Allow trainers to add sessions" + multi-trainer assignment — the event can start with zero sessions).
- **Announcements** (Notifications page), seven **filterable reports** with Excel/CSV/PDF export, and **Settings** (trainer seats, trainer-creation switch, working hours, branding, departments & categories, integrations, and Reset platform data).

## 8. FAQ

- **Where are my meeting links?** On each session row (Join) and in that session's Outlook invitation.
- **Why can't I see an event a colleague sees?** It's limited to their batch.
- **Am I registered for all sessions after Notify Me?** Yes — one registration covers the programme.
- **Registration is closed?** The event's validity window has passed — contact L&D.
- **Who resets passwords?** The administrator, from the Trainers / Team Leaders / Trainees pages.

## 9. Demo environment notes

Meeting links are generated by the portal in a realistic format (production would use each vendor's API). Outlook .ics invitations are real and import into Outlook. Azure AD SSO and SMTP activate when IT connects credentials.
